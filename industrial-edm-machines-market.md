<p><p>The industrial EDM (Electrical Discharge Machining) machines market is primarily categorized into two types: Sinker EDM and Wire EDM. Sinker EDM uses a shaped electrode to create complex cavities and contours in hard materials, ideal for die and mold making. In contrast, Wire EDM employs a thin, electrically charged wire to cut through materials with high precision, making it suitable for intricate shapes and tight tolerances. Both techniques are vital in manufacturing sectors requiring precision machining and efficient material removal.</p></p>
