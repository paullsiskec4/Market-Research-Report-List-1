<p><ul><li>Stiff Boom Cranes</li><li>Folding Boom Cranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wind crane market comprises two main types: stiff boom cranes and folding boom cranes. Stiff boom cranes feature a rigid, fixed boom that offers stability and precision for lifting heavy loads, ideal for tasks requiring maximum reach and load capacity. In contrast, folding boom cranes have a telescopic or articulated design, allowing for greater maneuverability and compact storage. These cranes are versatile for various applications, particularly in tight spaces or environments with height restrictions, enhancing their usability in wind energy projects.</p></p>
