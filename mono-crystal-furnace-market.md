<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Mono-Crystal Furnace Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Czochralski (CZ) Method Furnace</li><li>Floating Zone (FZ) Method Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mono-crystal furnace market primarily includes two types: the Czochralski (CZ) method furnace and the Floating Zone (FZ) method furnace. The CZ method involves pulling a seed crystal from a molten bath, producing large single crystals, mainly of silicon for the semiconductor industry. In contrast, the FZ method utilizes a localized heating zone to melt and refine materials in a controlled environment, allowing for high-purity crystal growth. Both methods cater to advanced applications in electronics, optics, and materials science.</p></p>
