<p><p>The Structural Insulated Panels (SIPs) market comprises various types, including EPS panels, PUR/PIR panels, glass wool panels, and others. EPS panels are lightweight and provide good insulation at a lower cost, making them popular for residential construction. PUR/PIR panels offer superior thermal performance and energy efficiency, making them ideal for commercial applications. Glass wool panels are environmentally friendly and provide excellent acoustic insulation.
