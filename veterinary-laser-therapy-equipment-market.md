<p><p>Veterinary laser therapy equipment is primarily categorized into Class 3B and Class IV lasers. Class 3B lasers deliver lower power levels, typically used for therapeutic purposes such as pain relief and inflammation reduction, suitable for small and medium-sized animals. In contrast, Class IV lasers provide higher power outputs, allowing for deeper tissue penetration and more effective treatments for a broader range of conditions, including post-surgical healing and chronic pain management. Both types enhance veterinary care through non-invasive treatment options.</p></p>
