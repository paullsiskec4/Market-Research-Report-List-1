<p><ul><li>X-ray Passenger Scanner</li><li>Millimeter Wave Passenger Scanner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The passenger scanner market comprises primarily two types: X-ray passenger scanners and millimeter wave passenger scanners. X-ray scanners utilize radiation to create images of luggage and body contents, focusing on identifying concealed items. They are commonly found in airports and secure facilities. In contrast, millimeter wave scanners utilize non-ionizing radio waves to produce detailed images of a person's body while maintaining privacy. These scanners are effective in detecting both metallic and non-metallic threats, enhancing security in public transportation hubs.</p></p>
