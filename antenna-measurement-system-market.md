<p><strong>The Antenna Measurement System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Near field Antenna Measurement System</li><li>Far-field Antenna Measurement System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Antenna Measurement System market is divided into near-field and far-field measurement systems. Near-field systems assess antenna characteristics in close proximity, utilizing techniques like spherical or planar scanning to capture detailed data on radiation patterns and efficiency. In contrast, far-field systems measure antenna performance at greater distances, ensuring accurate representation of how antennas function in real-world scenarios. Both types are essential for optimizing antenna design, performance validation, and compliance with industry standards in telecommunications and broadcasting.</p></p>
