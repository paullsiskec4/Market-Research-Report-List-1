<p><p>The bypass valves market is categorized into several types, each serving unique functions. Globe valves offer adjustable flow control, while piston valves are known for their robust sealing capabilities. Gate valves provide a straight flow path, minimizing pressure drop, whereas diaphragm valves excel in controlling flow with minimal contamination. Slide valves enable quick shutoff and are often used in isolation. Other types may include butterfly valves and check valves, catering to specialized applications across various industries.</p></p>
