<p><ul><li>Polymer Matrix Composite</li><li>Metal Matrix Composite</li><li>Ceramic Matrix Composite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The composite materials market encompasses three primary types: Polymer Matrix Composites (PMCs), which use a polymer resin as a matrix, offering lightweight and corrosion-resistant properties ideal for aerospace and automotive applications. Metal Matrix Composites (MMCs) combine metal matrices with reinforcements, enhancing strength and thermal conductivity, suitable for high-performance engineering. Ceramic Matrix Composites (CMCs) consist of ceramic matrices reinforced with fibers, providing high-temperature stability and abrasion resistance, making them essential in aerospace and industrial applications where durability is critical.</p></p>
