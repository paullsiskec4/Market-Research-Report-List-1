<p><ul><li>Pressure Sensitive</li><li>Glue-applied</li><li>Heat-Shrink and Stretch Sleeve</li><li>In-mold Labels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The offset print label market encompasses various types, each suited for specific applications. Pressure sensitive labels are adhesive-backed and easy to apply, making them popular for many products. Glue-applied labels are affixed using adhesive and are commonly used in beverage and food packaging. Heat-shrink labels conform to container shapes when heated, offering a snug fit and vibrant designs. Stretch sleeve labels stretch over the container and shrink upon application, providing a full-wrap look that enhances branding options.</p></p>
