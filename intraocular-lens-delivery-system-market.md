<p><p>The Intraocular Lens Delivery System market is categorized into two main types: preloaded and manually loaded systems. </p><p>Preloaded Intraocular Lens Delivery Systems come with the lens already loaded into the injector, ensuring ease of use and reducing the likelihood of contamination. </p><p>In contrast, manually loaded systems require the surgeon to load the lens into the injector before the procedure, which can provide flexibility but may increase the risk of handling errors. Both systems aim to facilitate cataract surgery and improve patient outcomes.</p></p>
