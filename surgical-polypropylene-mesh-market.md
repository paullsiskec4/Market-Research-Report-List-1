<p><ul><li>Non-absorbable Surgical Mesh</li><li>Absorbable Surgical Mesh</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The surgical polypropylene mesh market is categorized into two main types: non-absorbable surgical mesh and absorbable surgical mesh. Non-absorbable surgical mesh is designed for long-term support in various surgical procedures, maintaining its strength and integrity over time, making it ideal for hernia repairs and pelvic organ prolapse. In contrast, absorbable surgical mesh gradually degrades and is absorbed by the body, providing temporary support while allowing for natural tissue healing, commonly used in less invasive surgeries or in pediatric applications.</p></p>
