The market comprises several output types: </p><p>- **PNP Output**: This type sources current; it is preferred for applications where a positive voltage is needed for triggering devices. </p><p>- **NPN Output**: Unlike PNP, NPN sinks current, making it suitable for applications using lower voltage systems. </p><p>- **OSE Output**: Offering a combination of safety and versatility, OSE outputs are often used in more complex systems requiring integrated safety solutions.</p></p>
