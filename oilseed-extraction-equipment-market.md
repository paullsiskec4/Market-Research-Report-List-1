<p><p>The oilseed extraction equipment market comprises conventional extraction and ultrasonic extraction methods. Conventional extraction, often employing mechanical pressing and solvent extraction, is widely used for its efficiency and scalability. In contrast, ultrasonic extraction utilizes high-frequency sound waves to enhance oil yield and extraction speed, offering a more environmentally friendly alternative. Both methods serve distinct customer needs, with conventional techniques favored for large-scale operations, while ultrasonic extraction appeals to those seeking higher quality oils and reduced solvent use.</p></p>
