<p><ul><li>Vacuum Toilet System</li><li>Pressured Water Flushing Toilet System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rail Vehicle Sanitation System market encompasses two primary types: Vacuum Toilet Systems and Pressured Water Flushing Toilet Systems. Vacuum Toilet Systems utilize a vacuum mechanism to assist in waste removal, significantly reducing water consumption and the weight of the system, making it ideal for high-speed trains. In contrast, Pressured Water Flushing Toilet Systems operate using high-pressure water to flush waste, providing a more traditional approach but usually at a higher water usage. Both systems aim to enhance hygiene and passenger comfort in rail transport.</p></p>
