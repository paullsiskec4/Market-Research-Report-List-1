<p><p>The Real-time Radiography market encompasses two primary types of imaging technologies: Flat Panel Detector and Linear Array Detector. Flat Panel Detectors provide high-resolution images with digital processing capabilities, facilitating immediate feedback and precise diagnostics in various applications. In contrast, Linear Array Detectors utilize a series of sensors aligned in a linear fashion, enabling efficient image capture and offering benefits in speed and simplicity. Both technologies enhance the quality and efficiency of radiographic inspections in industrial and medical fields.</p></p>
