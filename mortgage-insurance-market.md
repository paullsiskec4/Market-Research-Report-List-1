<p><strong>The Mortgage Insurance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Borrower-Paid Mortgage Insurance</li><li>Lender-Paid Mortgage Insurance</li><li>FHA Mortgage Insurance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mortgage insurance protects lenders against default risks. Borrower-Paid Mortgage Insurance (BPMI) requires borrowers to finance the insurance premiums, typically added to monthly payments. Lender-Paid Mortgage Insurance (LPMI) shifts the responsibility to lenders, who cover the costs in exchange for slightly higher interest rates. FHA Mortgage Insurance is specific to loans backed by the Federal Housing Administration, requiring both upfront and monthly premiums from borrowers, ensuring broader access to home financing while protecting lenders.</p></p>
