<p><p>The titanium alloy scrap market comprises various types, each with distinct characteristics. Mixed titanium solids include various titanium components combined together. Titanium turnings are small, curled metal chips produced during machining processes. Titanium swarf refers to fine shavings or metal fragments generated from manufacturing. Rutile scraps consist of titanium dioxide materials. Titanium sponge/residues are porous titanium produced in processing. Other titanium scrap encompasses miscellaneous leftover titanium from different applications, contributing to recycling efforts and resource recovery in the industry.</p></p>
