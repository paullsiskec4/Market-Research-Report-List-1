<p><ul><li>Electronic Pad Wear Sensors</li><li>Audible Pad Wear Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brake pad wear sensors market comprises primarily two types: electronic pad wear sensors and audible pad wear sensors. Electronic pad wear sensors utilize electrical signals to detect the thickness of brake pads and alert the driver when they need replacement, enhancing safety and maintenance efficiency. In contrast, audible pad wear sensors emit a sound when the brake pads are worn down, providing a straightforward, cost-effective warning system. Both types play a crucial role in vehicle safety and performance monitoring.</p></p>
