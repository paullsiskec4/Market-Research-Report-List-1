<p><p>The Customized Gas Permeable Contact Lenses market comprises three primary types: spherical, toric, and multifocal lenses. Spherical lenses correct myopia or hyperopia, providing uniform vision correction. Toric lenses are designed for astigmatism, featuring different curvatures to address varying vision needs in different meridians. Multifocal lenses cater to presbyopia, allowing users to see clearly at multiple distances. Each type offers tailored solutions for individual vision requirements, enhancing comfort and visual performance for various eye conditions.</p></p>
