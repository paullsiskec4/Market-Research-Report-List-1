CT (Computed Tomography) devices provide rapid imaging for structural analysis, MRI (Magnetic Resonance Imaging) devices excel in soft tissue evaluation and brain function assessment. PET (Positron Emission Tomography) devices visualize metabolic processes, while PET-CT devices combine metabolic and structural imaging for enhanced precision. PET-MRI devices integrate both techniques, offering comprehensive insights into brain activity and anatomy, thus driving advancements in diagnosis and treatment of neurological conditions.</p></p>
