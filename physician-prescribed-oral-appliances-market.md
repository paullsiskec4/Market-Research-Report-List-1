<p><p>The physician-prescribed oral appliances market includes various types primarily focused on treating sleep-disordered breathing conditions, such as obstructive sleep apnea. Mandibular advancement devices (MADs) work by repositioning the jaw forward to keep the airway open during sleep. Tongue-retaining devices (TRDs), on the other hand, hold the tongue in a forward position to prevent it from obstructing the airway. Both types are customized by professionals, enhancing comfort and efficacy for patients seeking non-invasive alternatives to traditional therapies.</p></p>
