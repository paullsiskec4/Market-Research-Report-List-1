<p><p>The drug-eluting stent market consists of various types based on the specific drugs used for elution. Rapamycin-eluting stents utilize the immunosuppressant rapamycin to prevent restenosis by inhibiting cell proliferation. Paclitaxel-eluting stents release paclitaxel, an anticancer drug, targeting cell growth and promoting vessel healing. Everolimus-eluting stents, which deliver everolimus, a derivative of rapamycin, also focus on reducing restenosis. Other stents may include alternative drug formulations or combinations to enhance efficacy and minimize complications in vascular interventions.</p></p>
