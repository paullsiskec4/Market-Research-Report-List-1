<p><p>Benefits Administration Systems can be categorized into two main market types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based. On-Premise systems require installation on a company's servers, offering greater control and customization but demanding extensive IT resources for maintenance and updates. In contrast, Cloud-Based systems are hosted remotely, providing ease of access, scalability, and lower upfront costs. They allow for automatic updates and improved data security, making them appealing for businesses seeking flexibility and cost-effectiveness in managing employee benefits.</p></p>
