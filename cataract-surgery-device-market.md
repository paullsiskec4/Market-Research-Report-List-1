<p><p>The cataract surgery device market comprises several key types. Intraocular lenses (IOLs) are artificial lenses implanted to replace cloudy lenses. Ophthalmic viscoelastic devices (OVDs) maintain intraocular space and protect tissues during surgery. Phacoemulsification equipment utilizes ultrasound to break up cataracts for removal. Femtosecond laser equipment offers precision in cataract surgery by utilizing laser technology to perform incisions and soften cataracts. Together, these devices enhance surgical outcomes and patient recovery in cataract procedures.</p></p>
