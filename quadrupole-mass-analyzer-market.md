<p><p>The quadrupole mass analyzer market primarily includes single quadrupole and triple quadrupole systems. Single quadrupole mass analyzers are widely used for routine analysis and simple applications, offering basic mass-to-charge ratio selection. In contrast, triple quadrupole mass analyzers have enhanced sensitivity and specificity, making them ideal for complex analyses, such as quantifying trace substances in various samples.
