<p><p>The Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) devices market primarily consists of two types: fixed pressure CPAP devices and auto-adjusting CPAP devices. Fixed pressure CPAP devices deliver a constant airflow pressure, ensuring consistent treatment for patients with obstructive sleep apnea. In contrast, auto-adjusting CPAP devices automatically adjust the pressure throughout the night based on detected breathing patterns, providing personalized therapy. This differentiation allows patients and healthcare providers to choose devices that best fit individual comfort and treatment needs.</p></p>
