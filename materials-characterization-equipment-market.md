<p><p>Materials characterization equipment encompasses various tools used to analyze the physical and chemical properties of materials. The Hall Effect System measures electrical properties and charge carrier concentration in materials. Vibrating Sample Magnetometers assess magnetic properties by detecting the magnetic moment of samples. Probe Stations enable precise electrical measurements at the micro-scale, often for semiconductor devices. Other equipment includes techniques such as X-ray diffraction and thermal analysis, providing insights into material structure, composition, and behavior under various conditions.</p></p>
