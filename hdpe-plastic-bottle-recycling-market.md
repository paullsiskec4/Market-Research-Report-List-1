<p><ul><li>Chemical Recycling</li><li>Mechanical Recycling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HDPE plastic bottle recycling encompasses two primary market types: mechanical recycling and chemical recycling. Mechanical recycling involves sorting, cleaning, and shredding used HDPE bottles to create raw materials for new products. This method is efficient but can affect material quality. In contrast, chemical recycling breaks down HDPE into its basic monomers through chemical processes, allowing for the creation of high-quality recycled plastics without degradation. Both methods play integral roles in promoting sustainability and reducing plastic waste in the environment.</p></p>
