<p><p>The biological catalyst market comprises various enzyme types, each playing a crucial role in biochemical reactions. Oxidoreductases facilitate oxidation-reduction processes, while transferases transfer functional groups between molecules. Hydrolases catalyze hydrolysis reactions, breaking bonds with water. Isomerases convert molecules into isomers, altering their structural arrangement. Lyases break chemical bonds without water, often forming double bonds, whereas ligases join two molecules, requiring ATP. This diverse market supports applications across pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and food industries, driving innovation and development.</p></p>
