<p><p>Capacitive proximity switches are sensors that detect the presence of materials, either conductive or non-conductive, without direct contact. The market is divided into DC and AC types. DC type switches operate using direct current, offering faster response times and suitable for low-voltage applications. In contrast, AC type switches use alternating current, often preferred for high-voltage environments, providing robust performance in industrial settings. Both types are widely used in automation, material handling, and safety applications, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
