<p><p>Electrical Utility Task Vehicles (EUTVs) are classified into three main types: Hybrid Electric Vehicles (HEVs), Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs), and Fuel Cell Electric Vehicles (FCEVs). HEVs combine a conventional internal combustion engine with an electric propulsion system, offering flexibility and efficiency. BEVs rely solely on electric batteries for power, providing zero emissions and reduced operating costs. FCEVs utilize hydrogen fuel cells to generate electricity, offering extended range and quick refueling. Each type caters to specific utility needs, balancing performance and environmental sustainability.</p></p>
