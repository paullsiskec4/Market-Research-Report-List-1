<p><p>Safety Instrumented Systems (SIS) encompass various market types tailored for critical industrial safety. Safety Interlock Systems ensure that operational parameters are met before allowing equipment to function, preventing unsafe conditions. Safety Shutdown Systems (SSD) automatically terminate processes during hazardous situations to protect personnel and equipment. Other categories include emergency shutdown systems and fire and gas detection systems, which enhance safety measures. Collectively, these systems are vital for risk reduction and maintaining operational integrity in high-risk environments.</p></p>
