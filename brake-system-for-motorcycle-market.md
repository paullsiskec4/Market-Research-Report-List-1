<p><ul><li>Drum Brake</li><li>Disc</li><li>Linkage Brake</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The motorcycle brake system market features several types: drum brakes, disc brakes, and linkage brakes. Drum brakes use friction generated by shoes pressing against a rotating drum, providing reliable stopping power, especially in older models. Disc brakes offer superior performance with improved heat dissipation and responsiveness, leading to widespread adoption in modern motorcycles. Linkage brakes connect the front and rear systems, enhancing stability and control during braking. Each type caters to different riding preferences and performance requirements in the motorcycle industry.</p></p>
